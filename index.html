  • Promoting and supporting the establishment of a national union for the UAE libraries through joint collaboration among them. This will hugely contributes to the expansion of the library and information services scope for the UAE society.

  • Smart Library is a hub of Knowledge Resources in Arabic and English language. Knowledge resources will be in the form of eBooks, Publications, Articles, Journals, Physical Books, Reference Material and special collection converted from Physical to digital format.

  • A national electronic platform, seeks to support and promote the enablers and pillars of the industry and dissemination of knowledge among the members of the UAE community, through enhancing and presenting the intellectual and knowledgeable production in the United Arab Emirates, and to reinforcing its role in supporting the infrastructure of the competitive economy based on knowledge.

  • Providing national statistical data and indicators in the field of UAE libraries through effective cooperation and partnerships and use of the state-of-the-art technologies.

  • Create bibliographic database that it contains data about virtually every book and journal information relate to UAE.

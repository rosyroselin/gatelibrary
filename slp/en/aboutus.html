About Smart Library

Provision of an innovative platform, that facilitate for the employees of ministries and federal governmental authorities the ways of access and benefit from a huge collection of electronic knowledge sources in Arabic and foreign languages.

Depending on the declaration of His Highness Sheikh Khalifa bin Zayed Al Nahyan - may God protect him - of the year 2016 as the Year of Reading , and based on the proposals of the retreat of 100 launched by His Highness Sheikh Mohammed bin Rashid Al Maktoum - may God bless him - to discuss the most important ideas and initiatives to consolidate the reading as community, popular and youth habit in the community of UAE. The idea of Emirates smart library came to find an institutional and knowledgeable national framework to include the culture of reading among the employees of ministries and federal government authorities through the provision and availability of knowledge infrastructure of reading and creativity.

  • Strengthen of institutional supportive culture to establish the culture of reading and knowledge of all employees of ministries and federal government authorities.
  • Supporting and assisting the decisions makers in all ministries and federal government authorities, through providing of modern information sources with knowledgeable priority.
  • Provision of electronic infrastructure for reading and creativity in ministries and federal governmental authorities.
  • Supporting and promoting the innovative channels to access to the sources of knowledge
  • Encouraging innovation and creativity among employees of ministries and federal government authorities through support and promote the levels of arts and reading they have.
  • Contributing in achievement of the objectives and plans of the government in the converting to a competitive knowledge society.
  • Establishing and including the reading in the culture of ministries and federal governmental authorities.
  • Provision of knowledgeable enablers to promote and encourage reading of the employees of ministries and federal governmental authorities.
  • Reinforcement of frameworks of participation in the sources of knowledge between the employees of ministries and federal and governmental authoroties.
  • Facilitation the access to the sources of information and knowledge through an innovative electronic platform.

All employees of ministries and federal governmental authorities of all leading, supervisory and executive levels.

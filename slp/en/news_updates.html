In the Spotlight

THE CAIRO INTERNATIONAL BOOK FAIR 2016

Ministry of Culture, Youth and Community Development participates in the the Cairo International Book Fair at its forty seventh session on 27 January and until 10 February, with the participation of 9 different cultural institutions reflecting a comprehensive picture about the diversity and uniqueness of Emirati culture and its contents. The wing includes publications by the Ministry of Culture, Youth and Community Development, the National Archives, the Union of the Emirates Writers, Sultan bin Zayed Culture and Media center, Juma Al Majid Center for Culture and Heritage, Fujairah Culture and Media Authority, Culture and Science symposium, Department of Culture and Information - Government of Sharjah and the Emirates Publishers Association.
